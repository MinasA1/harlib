The status field indicates the instant in which the connection was requested. The format of this DateTime must be in full ISO 8601 format, i.e.: %Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%SZ (C/C++), yyyy-MM-dd'T'HH:mm:ss'Z' (Java), and must be expressed in the UTC timezone. The time field represents the total elapsed time in milliseconds (ms). For more information about time-related fields, see Timings objects.
